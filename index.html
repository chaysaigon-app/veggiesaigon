🌿 About Us
Welcome to Veggie Saigon – where the essence of Vietnamese culinary heritage meets international fusion in a Pure Vegan experience. 🌿

1. Strict Standards for Body & Soul:
We deeply respect your mindful dietary choices. The Veggie Saigon kitchen is committed to:
  • 100% Pure Vegan: No eggs, no animal-derived ingredients.
  • Allium-Free: Strictly no onions, garlic, leeks, shallots, or chives.
  • 100% Vegan Drinks: We use only natural plant-based milks; absolutely no dairy milk or honey.
2. Fresh Ingredients & Exquisite Selection:
We use only the freshest vegetables and fruits, hand-picked daily. Each dish is a skillful blend of traditional Vietnamese soul and international culinary fusion, offering flavors that are both familiar and sophisticated.

3. Diverse Menu – Perfect for Every Occasion:
Whether you are dining solo or with family, Veggie Saigon has the perfect choice:
  • Classic Vietnamese staples: Bánh mì, Phở, Vermicelli, and Noodle Soups.
  • Variety of styles: Crispy fried dishes, savory stir-fries, and elegant soups.
  • Full service: From light appetizers to cozy family-style meals.
4. Mindful Eating & Connection:
More than just a meal, we offer a space for Mindfulness. We invite our guests to not only enjoy delicious food but also to nourish peace within the soul and connect with the love of all living beings. 🙏
